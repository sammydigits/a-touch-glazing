About

Bathtub reglazing is by far the best solution for homeowners and rental properties.

Etch/epoxy primer/acrylic urethane top coat is the superior process for the perfect finish. Porcelain and enameled tubs will be properly abraded with our acid etching solution, followed by wet sanding with an 80 grit to ensure the best result desired in our prepping process.

Acrylic or Fiberglass tub and surrounds can be easily abraded by scuffing or sanding.

Now that we have the tub ready for painting we will then wipe out any residue with reducer to insure a cleaner surface. Following 3 coats of epoxy primer, each gradually thicker than the last and 3 coats of acrylic urethane with the same process giving you the best finish in the business.

Tiles on walls will have the same process from start to ending to insure quality of work. Some tubs will need to be strip from that failed coating.

We are certified professionals and pride ourselves on excellent customer service and competitive pricing.
